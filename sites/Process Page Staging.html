The Team Benchmark diagnostic purchase and survey process can be summed up in eight simple steps:

  • Step 1: Choose the diagnostic you wish to purchase.

  • Step 2: Select your team size and temporary team name.

  • Step 3: Complete the purchase process through your shopping cart.

  • Step 4: Activate your account by clicking on the link provided in your account confirmation email, and set your password. If you already have an account, log in to you account.

  • Step 5: Go the the My Benchmark page, which is available in the site navigation after logging in. On the My Benchmark page, click on the name of the team you selected during your purchase process.

  • Step 5: Assign team members for the diagnostic you have purchased, and send them invitations to complete he selected assignments.

  • Step 6: Have team members complete the diagnostic.

  • Step 7: Print completed team diagnostic report.

  • Step 8: Discuss the results and create an action plan.

  • Step 9: Implement plan and review progress.
