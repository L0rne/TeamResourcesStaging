The Registration Process

Team Benchmark Registration is quick and easy.

Click on the Registration link in the main menu, and fill out the form provided

Purchasing a Diagnostic

First select the diagnostic(s) you would like to purchase, and click on “Add to Cart”.

Once you have added a diagnostic to your cart, the shopping cart toolbar will appear at the bottom of your browser window. This will allow you to edit the items in your cart, or to “Proceed to Checkout”.

Once you’ve finished selecting diagnostics for your cart, click on “Proceed to Checkout”.

Provide your billing information, then click on “Continue to next step”.

On the next page, review your email address and billing information. If the email and billing information are correct, provide your credit card information, then click on “Continue to next step”.

You will be taken to the “Checkout Complete” Page.

From here you should check your email. If this is your first purchase from Team Benchmark, you will receive an email entitled “Account Created at Team Benchmark”. Simply follow the instructions in this email to log in to your account.

If you already have an account from a previous Team Benchmark purchase, you’ll receive an email informing you of your purchase. Simply log in to your current account, and you’ll see your new diagnostics available in the Administrator’s Control Panel.

Naming Your Team and Adding a Description

Once you’ve made a purchase on Team Benchmark and entered a password for your account, you’ll be automatically taken to the Benchmark Team Builder. This will walk you through the process of building a team, step by step.

The first step is to name and give a brief description of your team.

Then click on “Next, Add Team Members” to be taken to the "Team Building 101: Add Team Members" Page where you will be asked to provide your team members’ email addresses.

On this page simply add your team members email addresses one line at a time. Don’t worry if you don’t have all the needed addresses right now. You will be able to add more team members to this team later through the Administrator’s Interface.

If you have purchased more than one diagnostic, click on “Apply Your Diagnostic” to proceed to the next step where, if you have purchased multiple diagnostics, you will select which and how many diagnostics to send to your team first.

If you have purchased only one diagnostic, click on “Set a Due Date”.

Setting a Due Date

On this page, you’ll notice that the Team Benchmark site automatically provides a due date two weeks from the start date. If you would like to change the date, simply click on the date entry filed, and choose your preferred due date on the calendar provided. Note that this due date will not effect the functionality or availability of your test to you or your team members. It is simply intended as a courtesy to encourage your team members not to wait too long before completing their survey questions. When you are satisfied with your selected due date, click on “Send Invitations” to proceed to the next step.

Reviewing and customizing Team Member Survey Notifications

On this page you will be shown a preview of the email that will be sent to each of your team members, inviting them to participate in the Team Benchmark Survey your have selected. Here you are also given the opportunity to add a personal message to your team. To do so, simply type your message in the field provided. When you’re finished, click on “Send this Invitation and Activate the Survey” to complete the set-up wizard, and launch your diagnostic(s).

The number of team member survey invitations being sent will appear on the final Thank You page. You’re finished setting up your team and sending out your diagnostic(s). Click on “View the Team Administrator's Page" to go to the Team Administrator’s page where you can edit the name and description of your team, view how many unused and completed surveys you have available, follow the progress of your active surveys, activate any other diagnostics you have or will purchase, add and remove team members, and communicate with current team members, and view completed survey reports.

Meet the Team Administrator’s Page

On the Team Administrator’s Page you can edit the name and description of your team, view how many unused and completed surveys you have available, follow the progress of your active surveys, activate any other diagnostics you have or will purchase, add and remove team members, and communicate with current team members, and view completed survey reports.

Editing Your Team’s Name and/or Description from the Team Administrator's Page

To edit your team’s name, simple click on “edit” to the right of your team’s name.

Your team name will then appear in the same place as before, however it will change to become an editable form field.

Make the changes you desire, and click “Save”. If you decide you do not want to make changes, click “Cancel” to close the form field.

To edit your team’s description, simple click on “edit” to the right of your team’s description.

Your team description will then appear in the same place as before, however it will change to become an editable form field.

Make the changes you desire, and click “Save”. If you decide you do not want to make changes, click “Cancel” to close the form field.

Adding and Removing Team Members

Adding or removing Team members is an easy process. If you have not yet set up your first team, you’ll be adding team members using the Team Set-Up Wizard. View the “Building A Team Using the Team Set-Up Wizard” on this page for more details.

If you have already set up an initial team and would like to add or remove team members, you can do so easily from the Team Administrator’s Page.

First, log in to the Team Benchmark site

Upon logging in, you will immediately be taken to your My Benchmark Page. On your My Benchmark Page, click on "Go to the Team Administrator's Page"

Adding Team Members

Once you're on the Team Administrator's Page, click on “Add Team Members” and type the new team members’ email addresses in the field provided using one line per email. Use the Return key on your keyboard to move your cursor to each new line.

When you’ve finished adding and editing your new team member emails, click on “Add Email to Team”

Removing Team Members

Any team member who has not begun answering survey questions can be removed from your team. In the list of team member email addresses in your Team Member list on your Team Administrator's Page, any team member still eligible for removal from your team will have a red subtraction icon with the words “Remove Member” on the same line as their email address.

To remove this Team Member, simply click on “Remove Member”. If a Team Member has already begun answering survey questions, a grey shield icon with the words “Survey Started” will appear on the same line as the Team Member’s email address. This Team Member is ineligible for removal.
